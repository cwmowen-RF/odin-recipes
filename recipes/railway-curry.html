Railway Curry

Insert finished Image

Description

Classic Indian vegan curry served on the railways

Ingredients

  • Curry seeds, leaves and chillies
  • onions
  • keralan curry paste
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • 100 ml vege stock
  • Cooked (Cauliflower and potato)
  • Pickled onions
  • Creme Fraiche
  • Juliened Ginger

Steps

  1. In pan Temper seeds leaves and chillies in 50 ml oil
  2. Add onions to pan and temper
  3. Add Chopped tomatoes and gently fry
  4. Add keralan paste to pan and gently fry
  5. Add veg stock to pan and gently simmer
  6. Cook cauliflower and potato and add to pan
  7. Add ginger to pan, stir through and take off heat
  8. Serve curry with a pinch of pickled onions and a teaspoon of creme fraiche
