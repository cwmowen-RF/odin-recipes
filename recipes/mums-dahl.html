Description

The good stuff, DAHL

Ingredients

  • Curry seeds, leaves and chillies
  • onions
  • 100g garlic, 50g ginger - Grated
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 100 ml vege stock
  • Vine Tomato - Halved
  • 4L of Red Lentils
  • 10L of Water
  • Salt, White Pepper and veg stock
  • 100ml Oil

Steps

  1. Wash lentils relentlessly
  2. Add lentils and tomato to 10L cold water and bring to boil
  3. Boil for an hour or until al-dente
  4. Once al dente leave on low heat
  5. In seperat pan add 100ml Oil and temper ingredients in specific order
  6. Curry seeds, leaves, chillies, onion, garlic, ginger and garam masala
  7. Add tempering mix to dahl, then deglaze tempering pan with 100ml Veg stock and add to dahl
  8. Season to taste and leave in pan, covered, overnight to bring out flavours
  9. If still lacking salt, add more veg stock to dahl
