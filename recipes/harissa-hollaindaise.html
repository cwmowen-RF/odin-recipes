Harissa Hollaindaise

Insert finished Image

Description

Tangy vegan hollaindaise

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion diced
  • 100 ml white wine vinegar
  • 1 packet silk tofu
  • 1 tablespoon rose harissa paste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon
  • Salt and white pepper

Steps

  1. In pan reduce onions in vinegar until it smells slightly sweet
  2. Once reduced leave to cool
  3. Once cooled blitz onions with tofu, harissa paste, lemon juice and a pinch of salt and pepper
  4. Blitz until smooth consistency
  5. Season to taste with Salt, white pepper and lemon juice
  6. Blitz again before use as tofu sets
