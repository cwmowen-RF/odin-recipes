Description

Mexican Style vegan chilli made from TVP with a dark chocolate twist

Ingredients

  • 500g TVP
  • 1L Water
  • 1 Large Onion
  • Herbs and Spices
  • 1 Tablespoon garlic puree
  • 28g Dark Chocolate

Steps

  1. Bring Water to boil and add TVP, then keep on low heat
  2. In Seperate Pan, temper 1 large onion in 100ml oil until translucent
  3. Add herbs and spices and cook for 1 minute
  4. Add onions to tvp pan
  5. Add garlic puree and continue to stir for 5 minutes
  6. Add Dark Chocolate, stir for 1 minute then take off heat
