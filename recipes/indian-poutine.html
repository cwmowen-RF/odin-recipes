Description

The hangover cure, dahl, cheese and chips

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl chips
  • 100g grated cheese
  • 50g dahl
  • 25ml veg stock
  • Mango Masala Mayonaisse
  • Tamarind sauce
  • Patcha

Steps

  1. Cook chips and place into ovenable bowl
  2. Top with cheese and cook in oven until melted
  3. In saucepan, mix dahl and vegstock until gravylike consistency
  4. Top cheesy chips with indian gravy
  5. Dress with tight lines of mango mayo and tamarind
  6. Top off with a tablespoon of patcha
